General FAQ’s

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Taam.ai?

    Taam.ai is an AI-powered platform providing tools for text, image, video, voice, and content generation through a unified API.

  • What AI features does Taam.ai offer?

    It includes Text Generator, Image Generator, Text-to-Image, Voiceover, Text-to-Video, Speech-to-Speech, Voice-to-Text, Image-to-Text, Conversational AI, and Premium Content access.

  • How do I create an account?

    Simply sign up on the Taam.ai website to access all tools, manage your dashboard, and personalize your AI experience.

  • Can I generate images from text prompts?

    Yes! Use the Text-to-Image or Image Generator tools to create high-quality visuals instantly from your text descriptions.

  • Does Taam.ai support voice generation?

    Yes, you can convert text to professional-quality voiceovers, adjust tone and speed, and even use Speech-to-Speech features.

  • Can I convert speech to text?

    Absolutely. The Voice-to-Text feature transcribes your spoken words into editable text accurately and in multiple languages.

  • Does Taam.ai provide plagiarism checking?

    Yes, you can verify the originality of your content with the built-in Plagiarism Checker to ensure authenticity.

  • Can I generate videos from text?

    Text-to-Video is a feature that transforms scripts into videos with visuals, voiceovers, and background music (if available in your plan).

  • Are there customization options for outputs?

    Yes, you can customize styles, sizes, tones, voices, and formats across images, videos, and voice outputs.

  • Can I use Taam.ai for multiple languages?

    Yes, several features like Voice-to-Text, Speech-to-Speech, and Image-to-Text support multiple languages.

  • How secure is my data on Taam.ai?

    All your data, content, and account information are protected using secure storage and encryption protocols.

  • Can developers integrate Taam.ai into their applications?

    Yes, Taam.ai provides a unified API to access all AI features programmatically, making it easy to integrate into apps and workflows.
